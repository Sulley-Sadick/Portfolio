About

I’m Sadick Sulley, a front-end developer with a strong interest in building responsive, accessible, and user-focused web applications. My introduction to software engineering came through a close friend who was studying computer science and often spoke about the depth and opportunities in the field. What began as curiosity quickly turned into genuine interest as I started exploring how software—and the web in particular—solves real problems. Over time, I found myself especially drawn to web development because of its direct impact on users. I’ve since been intentional about learning how to build interfaces that are not only visually sound, but also functional, performant, and accessible across devices.
